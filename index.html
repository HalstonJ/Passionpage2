Boston Terriers

Boston terriers are a breed of dog that originated in the U.S. from the results of mixing an English Bulldog and a white English Terrier. They are loveable and extremely energetic little dogs, that love attention and play. However, before you consider getting one there are some factors in deciding whether a Boston Terrier would be right for you.

Pros
Energetic little goobers
Smaller, easier to travel with
Very friendly

Cons
Due to their muzzle shape, their breathing is difficult
More susceptible to health issues

Before deciding on a boston terrier, it is optimal to research more about their needs and health issues. Such as their health, what to feed them, how to take care of their social needs, and many more. Boston terriers have been my favorite breed of dog because of their energy and awesome personalities.
